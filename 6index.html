About Us

GalaxyDrive is a student-driven team focused on building practical, user-friendly digital solutions that solve real-world problems. Our goal is to combine clean design with simple technology to create applications that are easy to use and genuinely helpful for everyday users.


Future Vision:

In the future, GalaxyDrive aims to grow into a unified platform that promotes both tourism and local food culture. Along with food spots and restaurant menus, we plan to include nearby tourist attractions such as temples, waterfalls, viewpoints, heritage places, and popular hangout locations. Users will be able to explore a location and see what to visit and where to eat in one place. For tourists and new visitors, this will make trip planning easier by providing spot timings, best visiting hours, food recommendations, and direct Google Maps navigation.
